# Brand Voice Builder

**Trigger**: "build brand voice", "create brand identity", "define tone", "build my voice"

## Description

Builds your product/company brand voice for marketing. Produces `about-brand.md` and `brand-voice.md` that all marketing skills will read.

## Step 1: Brand Interview

Ask the Owner these questions:

1. **Product/Company Name**: What is it called?
2. **What does it do?**: In one sentence, what's the core value?
3. **Who is it for?**: Founders, devs, marketers, enterprises...
4. **What makes you different?**: What do you believe that others don't?
5. **What will you NEVER communicate?**: Forbidden topics
6. **Brand Personality**: Pick one:
   - **Technical & Direct**: Like documentation that talks
   - **Warm & Human**: Like a friend who knows a lot
   - **Bold & Disruptive**: Like someone who breaks rules
   - **Premium & Elegant**: Like accessible luxury

## Step 2: Create about-brand.md

```markdown
# About [Product Name]

## Name
[Product name + tagline]

## What it does
[One sentence value proposition]

## Audience
[Who this is for, in 2-3 sentences]

## Content pillars
- [Topic 1]
- [Topic 2]
- [Topic 3]
- [Topic 4]

## Point of view
[Contrarian belief or distinctive angle]

## What we DO NOT communicate
[Off-limits topics]
```

## Step 3: Analyze Samples

Ask for 3-5 examples of:
- LinkedIn posts from competitors you admire
- Your own content if it exists
- Tone from other brands you like

## Step 4: Create brand-voice.md

```markdown
# Brand Voice Profile

## How our brand sounds
[2-3 sentences]

## Tone
- ✅ [3-5 attributes we hit]
- ❌ [1-2 tones we NEVER hit]

## Writing rhythm
[Sentence length, paragraph structure, pacing]

## Hook patterns
[3-5 hook types we use, with examples]

## How we open
[Opening style]

## How we close
[Closing style + CTA approach]

## Signature phrases
[Recurring words or phrases]

## What this brand NEVER does
[3-5 specific behaviors to avoid]
```

## Step 5: Confirm

> Your brand profile is ready. Two files created:
> - `about-brand.md`: who you are, audience, pillars
> - `brand-voice.md`: how you write, what you avoid
>
> Now you can say:
> - "Write a post about [topic]" → post-writer
> - "Give me content ideas" → content-matrix
> - "I need hooks for [topic]" → hook-generator
> - "Create a Twitter thread" → thread-writer

## Rules
- Max 300 words in about-brand.md
- Max 500 words in brand-voice.md
- No generic filler. Everything based on real samples.
- English unless Spanish is specified.
