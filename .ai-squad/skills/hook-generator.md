# Hook Generator

**Trigger**: "give me hooks", "hook ideas", "I need a hook for", "generate hooks", "openers"

## Description

Generates 6 clickbait hook variations for any topic. Formula: opening line (40 chars) + contrast line (40 chars).

## Step 1: Get Topic

If Owner already gave a topic, use it. If not, ask:
> What topic do you want hooks for?

## Step 2: Write 6 Variations

Each hook has:
- **Line 1**: Max 40 characters. No questions. Something unexpected/specific.
- **Line 2**: Max 40 characters. Contradicts or reframes line 1.

Requirements:
- At least one "How I" or "I" statement across the two lines
- Include digits/metrics where possible
- Tension, curiosity, stakes

6 angles:
1. **Number-led**: Lead with number or metric
2. **Contrarian**: Belief then flip
3. **Personal transformation**: Before vs after with digit
4. **Authority steal**: Reference name, tool, brand
5. **Admission**: Confess mistake or loss
6. **Future shock**: Prediction or "X is about to change"

## Step 3: Output

```
HOOKS for [topic]

1. [Number-led]
[Line 1]
[Line 2]

2. [Contrarian]
[Line 1]
[Line 2]

3. [Personal transformation]
[Line 1]
[Line 2]

4. [Authority steal]
[Line 1]
[Line 2]

5. [Admission]
[Line 1]
[Line 2]

6. [Future shock]
[Line 1]
[Line 2]
```

## Step 4: Next Step

> Which one should I develop into a full post? Use post-writer with the hook number.

## Rules
- 40 characters max per line. COUNT THEM.
- No questions in opening line
- No filler. Every word earns its place
- Prefer digits over spelled words (3, not three)
